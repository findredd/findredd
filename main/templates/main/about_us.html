About us

We are glad to have you here and here are a few things about us that we would love to share with you.

What is FindREDD all about?

Findredd is an innovative approach to address global health. We provide immediate access to blood donors all over India, 24/7. Findredd is working in partnership with several organizations that work as a network and respond to disasters or emergency situations in an efficient manner.

What we do?

The ultimate goal of our organisation is to provide an easy-to-use, easy-to-access, efficient, and reliable way to get life-saving blood, free of cost. Findredd works with Online Management System to connect blood donors and recipients through our Website or our mobile application.

How it works?

Our automated system works efficiently whenever someone needs to search for blood. Simply Search and get in touch with a blood donor, either on the website itself or by downloading our Android app. Instead of having to search all over for a blood donor in an emergency situation, you can Call our Helpline numbers and get direct help.

Our Origin

FINDREDD was initially named BIETian blood bank and was formed in December 2019 by some students of BIET Jhansi. In it’s earlier days, the organisation worked manually and we succeeded in convincing many people to donate blood. The organisation has now been registered under the name FINDREDD as we aim to expand its scope of service.

Our Aim

We wanted to use this world of science, technology and advancements for saving lives and with this objective in mind, we decided to give our organisation a platform and so here we are.

Services are absolutely free

Our services are absolutely free. This organisation is a platform for voluntary service and hence no give and take of money is allowed.
