FindREDD for life

The ultimate goal of our organization is to provide an easy-to-use, easy-to-access, efficient, and reliable way to get life-saving blood, free of cost.

What is FindREDD?

Findredd is an innovative approach to address global health. We provide immediate access to blood donors all over India, 24/7.

Meet our heroes

We are proud of our heroes who have devoted their lives to the selfless service of humanity.

Brijesh Yadav→

Talking about the experience while donating, it was not at all scary. I myself donated 700ml of plasma. It’s a myth that a person feels weak after donating blood or plasma. Donating a unit of blood takes just five minutes and makes you a reason for someone else’s priceless smile.

Kaif Khan→

It was a great experience. Neither did I feel any weakness nor any pain. I always wanted to donate my blood and help a needy person by saving his life. Everyone must donate blood every 4 months so that we can perpetuate this selfless service and extend help to the needy.

Manas Goel→

At first I was bit scared because I was donating blood for the first time but I was also very happy at the same time. I just want to save a life if I can.

Bipin Kumar→

I felt relaxed & extremely happy during the process. If our life & body can save someone else’s life then that is the greatest achievement of our body & life.

Vivek Gupta→

A friend's father was in critical condition and he needed blood. I thought that I must help him and hence I decided to donate blood to him. At first I was a bit scared but as the time passed, I realized that it was not a very scary thing at all. I believe that everyone should donate blood at least once in six months.

Harshit→

My uncle was suffering from relapsed blood cancer and we required a lot of blood. The donors are real heroes and healthy people should donate blood from time to time. I'm extremely thankful to all the people who stepped up and donated blood to my uncle.
